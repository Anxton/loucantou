Whether for a night, a weekend or a week, Andrea Kutzke welcomes you to Lou Cantou Bed & Breakfast, in her 1825 manor house located in the heart of the village of Caux in the Hérault.

In summer, breakfast is served on the terrace. Dogs are also welcome. Internet access is available in both rooms. We speak English und problemlos Deutsch!

L'olivier

Composed of two connected rooms, this 50m² family room can comfortably accommodate 2 to 5 people.
On the ground floor, it has a spacious private bathroom and a shared kitchen: a suite in the reception rooms of the fully renovated 1825 manor house.
Internet access.

The living room can be converted into a bedroom for 3 additional guests (a single bed, a BZ sofa) and features a marble mosaic floor.
